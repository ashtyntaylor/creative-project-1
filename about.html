Hi! My name is Khloe and this is my closet! You're probably wondering what this website is all about. Well, let me explain. This project originally started as a simple way for me to clean out my closet and get rid of all the clothes that I was no longer wearing. I'd post the articles of clothing on my Insta and then sell them to whoever commented first. It has sense evolved into what it is today - a thrifting website!

Still don't get it? Basically, I do all of the hard work of digging through the old, grungy clothes that you find at typical thrift stores, to find the few hidden gems. Then I gather all of my finds here, just for you. Now you can find all of your favorite brands at thrifted prices without any of the hassle. Enjoy!
